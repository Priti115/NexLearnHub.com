MEDICAL SCIENCE

Medical science, also known as medicine or biomedical science, is a branch of science that deals with the study of the human body, diseases, and the practice of diagnosing, treating, and preventing illnesses. It encompasses a wide range of disciplines and fields dedicated to understanding the structure and function of the human body, as well as the mechanisms behind diseases and their treatment.

COMPUTER SCIENCE

Computer science is a field of study that encompasses the theory, experimentation, and engineering related to computers and computational systems. It involves understanding the principles underlying computer systems, designing and building software and hardware systems, and applying computational thinking to solve real-world problems.
